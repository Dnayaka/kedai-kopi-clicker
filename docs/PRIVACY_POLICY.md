# Privacy Policy — Kedai Kopi Clicker

**Last updated:** 2026-07-15

Kedai Kopi Clicker ("the App") is developed by dnayaka ("we", "us"). This
policy explains what happens with your data when you use the App.

## Short version

**The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** It has no
server, no account system, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything the
App needs is stored only on your own device.

## What data the App stores

The App saves your game progress (coins, buildings/upgrades owned, mission
progress, your chosen café name, and your in-app settings such as sound,
music, graphics quality, and camera sensitivity) locally on your device using
your browser/WebView's `localStorage`. This data:

- Never leaves your device.
- Is not sent to us or to any third party.
- Is not linked to your identity, email, or any account.
- Is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App, or manually if you use
  the in-app "Mulai dari Awal Lagi" (Start Over) option in Settings.

## Permissions the App uses

- **Notifications** (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`): used only to show a local
  "your coffee is piling up" reminder if you enable it in Settings. This
  notification is generated and scheduled entirely on your device — no data
  is sent anywhere to produce it, and you can disable it at any time.

The App does **not** request internet access, location, camera, microphone,
contacts, storage, or any other permission.

## Third-party services

None. The App does not integrate any third-party SDK — no analytics, no
crash reporting, no advertising network, no social login.

## Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children,
because it does not collect data from anyone at all.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes (e.g. because a future update adds a feature
that uses data differently), the "Last updated" date above will change and
the new policy will describe exactly what changed.
